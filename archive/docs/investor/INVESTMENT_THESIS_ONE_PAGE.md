# SoftFocus — One-Page Investment Thesis

## Overview

SoftFocus is a governed analytical product
operating in the domain of cognitive load,
burnout risk signals, and recovery capacity.

It is explicitly non-clinical and informational.

---

## Core Thesis

SoftFocus is not optimized for rapid growth.
It is optimized for **control, trust, and monetizable clarity**.

Revenue is a controlled outcome,
not a forcing function.

---

## Product Characteristics

- Deterministic analytical core
- Frozen logic under governance
- Clear non-clinical boundary
- Orientation-first user experience
- One-time analytical products

---

## Monetization Model

- Price range: $29–39
- No subscriptions
- No retention pressure
- Monetization gated by governance

Revenue can be enabled within 24 hours
after explicit approval.

## Risk Profile

- Low legal exposure
- No outcome promises

## Investment Rationale

SoftFocus represents a **controlled analytical asset**:
- monetizable on demand
- auditable by design
- resilient to hype cycles

This is not a growth bet.
