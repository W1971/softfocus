# Board-Level Due Diligence Checklist

## Governance & Control
- [ ] Project lifecycle defined and enforced
- [ ] Phase C governance active
- [ ] Monetization gated by approval
- [ ] Revocation mechanism present

---

## Product Integrity
- [ ] Core logic deterministic and frozen
- [ ] No adaptive or manipulative behavior
- [ ] Clear non-clinical boundary
- [ ] Mobile-first orientation respected

---

## Monetization Readiness
- [ ] Dry-run completed or available
- [ ] Approval record required
- [ ] 24h enablement runbook documented
- [ ] Rollback path verified

---

## Metrics & Forecasting
- [ ] Shadow metrics in place
- [ ] No real payments executed
- [ ] Revenue projections expressed as ranges
- [ ] Assumptions explicitly documented

---

## Legal & Risk
- [ ] Legal FAQ available
- [ ] Refund policy defined (future-ready)
- [ ] No subscriptions or recurring billing
- [ ] Audit trail maintained

---

## Investor Communication
- [ ] Verbal script aligned with governance
- [ ] FAQ addresses monetization delay
- [ ] No revenue promises made
- [ ] Data Room complete and indexed

---

## Board Statement

The system demonstrates
operational readiness with deliberate restraint.

Monetization is a decision,
not a dependency.
