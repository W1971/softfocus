## Scope

are included in the sitemap during Phase D.

## Included (indexable)

- pages/en/burnout-test-for-*
- status: indexable
- phase: phase-d or earlier
- pSEO entry pages only

- burnout-test-for-consultants

---

## Excluded

- any page with status: draft
- calculators (all variants)
- guides and explanations
- internal review or experiment pages
- non-English locales until explicitly approved

---

## Linking Rules

- pSEO pages may link only to:
  - /{locale}/tests/burnout-test
- No cross-linking between role pages
- No links from homepage or calculators

---

## Governance

Any sitemap change requires:
- explicit approval
- phase marker
- documentation update
