# SoftFocus — Assessment Question Bank

Version: v1.0  
Status: LOCKED  
Scope: Canonical assessment questions

---

## Purpose

Define a **stable, professional question set**
that positions the assessment as an analytical product,
not a subjective self-evaluation.

---

## Global Rules

All questions:

- reference observable behavior
- avoid emotional language
- avoid categorical self-attribution
- include temporal anchoring
- use neutral, professional tone

---

## Canonical Questions (20)

### Decision Load

1. In the past 14 days, how often have you made important decisions while cognitively fatigued?
2. How frequently do decisions require noticeably more effort than they used to?
3. How often do you postpone decisions due to lack of mental clarity?

### Cognitive Sustainability

4. In the last two weeks, how stable has your ability to focus across a full workday been?
5. How often do you rely on compensating strategies (lists, repetition, double-checking) to maintain accuracy?
6. How frequently do you feel mentally “spent” earlier than expected?

### Recovery Efficiency

7. Over the past 14 days, how effective has your non-working time been in restoring mental capacity?
8. How often does rest fail to reduce cognitive strain?
9. How frequently do you return to work already mentally taxed?

### Attention Fragmentation

10. In the last two weeks, how often has sustained focus been interrupted by internal distraction?
11. How frequently do you switch tasks due to declining concentration rather than priority changes?
12. How often do you re-read or re-check information to maintain comprehension?

### Load Compensation

13. How often do you compensate declining energy with increased effort?
14. In the last 14 days, how frequently have you extended work time to preserve output quality?
15. How often do you feel performance is maintained “at a cost”?

### Error Sensitivity

16. How frequently do small mistakes require correction after completion?
17. In the last two weeks, how often has decision confidence declined after the fact?
18. How often do you notice delayed recognition of errors?

### Sustainability Signal

19. How stable does your current workload feel over the next 4–6 weeks?
20. How often do you question whether your current pace is sustainable?
