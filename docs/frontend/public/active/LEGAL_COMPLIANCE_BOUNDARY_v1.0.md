> **Scope & Compliance Boundary**  
> This document may reference clinical terminology **solely to clarify scope exclusion**.  
> SoftFocus does **not** provide diagnosis, treatment, or therapeutic services,  
> and is not a medical or mental health system.

# Legal & Compliance Boundary

Version: v1.0  
Status: ACTIVE  
Audience: Public  
Purpose: Legal clarity & risk limitation

---

## Non-Clinical Nature

SoftFocus is not a medical device,
medical service, or psychological diagnostic tool.

It does not diagnose,
treat, or prevent medical conditions.

---

## Informational Use Only

All assessment outputs are provided
for **informational and awareness purposes only**.

They are not intended to replace
professional medical, psychological,
or occupational health advice.

---

## Employment & HR Boundary

SoftFocus is not designed to:

- evaluate individual performance,
- support employment decisions,
- justify disciplinary actions.

B2B use is limited to **anonymized, aggregated insights**.

---

## Data & Privacy Principles

- no sale of personal data,
- anonymized by default,
- minimal data collection,
- purpose-limited use.

---

## User Responsibility

If a user experiences:

- acute distress,
- functional impairment,
- health or safety concerns,

professional clinical support is appropriate.

---

## Jurisdiction Note

SoftFocus operates as a non-clinical,
informational assessment platform.
Local regulations may vary.
